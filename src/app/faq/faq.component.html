Frequently Asked Questions

ORDER PLACEMENT

How can I track my order?

At every step of the way you will be receiving information of the progress of your order.

How do I place orders with Aswikamart?

In 4 simple steps you can place your orders with us.
• Browse through our categories
• Add products to the shopping bag
• Register with your email, address, and contact details.
• Confirm and Pay for the orders.

Why is the registration necessary?

We recommend you to register with us with your contact details as that makes it easier to keep track of all your activities on OhhoExpress, also it enables us to keep you informed about new offers and deals as our valued customer.

How do I know that my order is confirmed?

When you successfully pay for your order at your check out, you will be taken to an order confirmation page where you will shown your order details as well as your order ID. You will also be receiving a confirmation mail from our end right after.


PAYMENTS

How do I pay for my orders?

We have different gateways for your convenience where from you can pay through any online payment options.


REFUND

How do I receive a refund?

If there is a product which is Out of Stock or not available at the moment due to unavoidable circumstances the amount for the product will be refunded to your Aswikamart Wallet.


MODIFICATION OF ORDER

Can the shipping address be modified after placing the order?

You can modify your shipping address before we start packaging your order. You can do that by clicking “Change Address” under the “My Order” section.
